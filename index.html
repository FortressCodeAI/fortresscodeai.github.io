How It Works

Compliance enforced at the execution layer

Most compliance tools operate above your AI system. BaseLayerOS operates underneath it — at the substrate — so governance properties are structurally true, not just documented.

01
Deploy the Substrate

BaseLayerOS sits underneath your AI model. Every action passes through the deterministic execution engine before and after it runs. No bypass path exists.

02
Select Your Pack

Choose the compliance pack matching your regulatory exposure. Each pack configures the governance envelope for your specific use case and vertical.

03
Actions Are Governed

Every action is validated, invariant-checked, and logged before state advances. Refusals are recorded as first-class artifacts — visible, reproducible, inspectable.

04
Audit On Demand

Any execution path can be replayed exactly. Regulators get deterministic proof — same input, same output, same refusal — not a retrospective documentation exercise.

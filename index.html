How It Works

Compliance enforced at the execution layer

Most compliance tools operate above your AI system. BaseLayerOS operates underneath it — so governance properties are structurally true, not just documented.

01
Deploy the Substrate

BaseLayerOS sits underneath your AI model. Every action passes through the deterministic execution engine. No bypass path exists.

02
Select Your Pack

Choose automated packs for continuous runtime governance or governance modules for fixed-scope compliance deliverables. Or both.

03
Actions Are Governed

Every action is validated, invariant-checked, and logged. Refusals are recorded as first-class artifacts — visible, reproducible, inspectable.

04
Audit On Demand

Any execution path can be replayed exactly. Regulators get deterministic proof — not a retrospective documentation exercise.
